# 🦬 Buffalo: The Drinking Game

## What Is Buffalo?

Buffalo is a classic social drinking game built on one simple rule:

> **You must always drink with your non-dominant hand. If you're caught using your dominant hand, someone calls "Buffalo" — and you finish your drink.**

That's it. No cards, no dice, no setup. Just awareness, quick reflexes, and the eternal vigilance of your friends.

---

## The Official Rules

1. **Declare your dominant hand** — everyone at the table should know which hand is your dominant hand before play begins.
2. **Always drink with your non-dominant hand** — this applies at all times, for any beverage, in any location.
3. **Getting Buffalo'd** — if you're caught drinking with your dominant hand (or using both hands), any player can call "Buffalo."
4. **The penalty** — if Buffalo is called correctly, you must finish your drink.
5. **False call** — if you call Buffalo incorrectly, *you* finish yours.

---

## Common Variations

Every group has their own twist. Popular variations include:

- **Sips instead of finishing** — agree on a set number of sips as the penalty
- **Alcoholic drinks only** — Buffalo doesn't apply to water or non-alcoholic beverages
- **Holding counts** — you can be Buffalo'd for holding a drink in your dominant hand, even without drinking
- **Immediate calls only** — Buffalo must be called the moment the drink touches your lips
- **No hand-switching** — once you declare your dominant hand, it's locked in for the night

All variations should be agreed upon **before play begins**.

## Play Responsibly

Buffalo is meant to be fun. A few reminders:

- The game is **opt-in** — never pressure anyone to participate
- Water and non-alcoholic drinks are always allowed, no exceptions
- Respect personal limits — knowing when to stop is part of the game
- Always have a plan to get home safely
